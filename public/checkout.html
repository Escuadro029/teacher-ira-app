📬 How File Delivery Works

When a buyer submits an order, it is saved as pending. After you verify their GCash payment, click Send Files in the Orders page.

🗂️ OneDrive Setup

Upload your ZIP files to OneDrive. Right-click → Share → Anyone with link → Copy. Add ?download=1 to the end and paste into the product's OneDrive Link field.

🐘 PostgreSQL Database

Products and orders are stored in your Render PostgreSQL database.
